Check:
(1) A move that attacks the opponent's king

Checkmate:
(1) A check that cannot be escaped

Chess notation:
(1) A language for writing down chess moves and recording a chess game

Combination:
(1) A tactic that consists of two or more moves

Endgame:
(1) The third phase of a chess game, when queens have been traded and queens are no longer on the board

Middlegame:
(1) The second phase of a chess game, after the players have finished their development

Opening:
(1) The first phase of a chess game, where both players develop their pieces

Strategy:
(1) A plan
(2) The part of chess concerned with planning

Tactic:
(1) A short sequence of moves that yields an advantage or a winning advantage
